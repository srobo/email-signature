How do I use this?

  1. Highlight everything below the line of 'T's
  2. Press ctrl/cmd+c
  3. Open your signature settings in your mail client in gmail it's this page
  4. Paste it into your 'Signature' box in your mail client
  5. Change the details to suit you, then click save!
